JANN is the open-source analytical publication of Applied Information Services.

Published under The AEROSINT — a Substack imprint — JANN tracks aerospace activity in the defense and intelligence operating environment using publicly available information, signals analysis, and primary source corroboration.

What Is AEROSINT

AEROSINT is the disciplined application of OSINT methodology to aerospace platforms, flight operations, and civil aviation infrastructure with defense or intelligence relevance.

The term was coined and codified by Applied Information Services. It represents a distinct analytical discipline — not a tool, not a product category, and not a casual observation of interesting aircraft. AEROSINT operates at the intersection of collection tradecraft, pattern-of-life analysis, and domain expertise.

Relationship to AIS

JANN functions as AIS’s published analytical record. Where AIS engagements are client-privileged, JANN represents the open-source layer: verified findings, documented methodologies, and signals of record for the defense and intelligence community.

Subscribers include analysts, contractors, program managers, and researchers operating across the defense industrial base and the intelligence community.
