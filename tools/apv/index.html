APV

APV (Applied Flight Visualizer) is an interactive flight-route visualization tool wrapping the open-source flightcn component set. Route arcs are rendered as great circles over a MapLibre basemap. Enter endpoints as IATA codes (LAX), ICAO codes (KJFK, EGLL, KDOV), or raw lng,lat coordinates. The bundled database covers ~4,400 major civilian airports and ~570 military installations worldwide (marked ★ in the airport list). All data shown here is illustrative only and does not represent real-time flight operations or operational intelligence.
